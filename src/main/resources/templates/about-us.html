We Produce Seed For Your Future Use

Seed banks play a critical role in preserving plant genetic diversity . we collect seeds from different regions and ecosystems, and store them under controlled conditions to prevent genetic erosion and ensure their long-term viability.

Crop Improvement

Seed banks contribute to the development of new and improved crop varieties by providing genetic resources for breeding programs. By selecting and crossing different plant varieties with desirable traits, breeders can develop new crops that are more resistant to pests, diseases.

Certificates

ISTA accreditation ensures that the seed bank follows rigorous procedures for seed testing, including purity,viability assessments. It also emphasizes the proper documentation and labeling of seed samples.
